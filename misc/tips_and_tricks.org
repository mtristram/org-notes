* Network
** Port forward
At home, the association between =xgarrido.dyndns.org= address with the local IP
must be done by adding

#+BEGIN_SRC sh
192.168.1.77 xgarrido.dyndns.org
#+END_SRC

in =/etc/hosts= file.
